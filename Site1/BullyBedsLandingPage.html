Which dogs need large dog beds or big dog beds?

A good rule of thumb to figure out if your furry friend needs a large dog bed is to observe how they prefer to sleep or rest, then measure the space. Dogs who prefer to stretch out need large dog beds, while those who like to curl may need one a bit more snug.

How do you clean large dog beds?

Our large dog beds all come with waterproof covers, making it a breeze to wipe off and clean up after your furry friends.

When it is time for a thorough wash, you can easily remove the covers, pop them in the washing machine and tumble with cold water. When it is time to dry the large dog bed, you can put them in the dryer on low heat or air dry.

What types of material are used in big dog beds?

Our Bully Beds all come with Certipur US Certified orthopedic foam that are non-hazardous, that each vary in thickness and dimensions based on the type of model.

All the large dog beds have covers that are washable and removable, and also vary in materials. Some are made with microfiber, others with Sherpa, still others with faux fur, and one option with a heavy-duty chew-resistant fabric.

How soft are the big dog beds?

Most of our large dog beds feel sturdy and durable, while our Sherpa top and Faux Fur options offer extra softness and comfort.

What is the best place to put a large dog bed?

The best place to put large dog beds is by the wall or in a corner. This helps dogs feel more secure compared to open spaces. Never put the bed in the middle to avoid accidents in the dark or waking them up with noise.
