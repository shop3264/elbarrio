HOW IT WORKS

A 15% fee is collected on every buy, sell, and transfer of $ATOM. A portion of this fee gets redistributed in to the liquidity pool,  to buy $UNIV for reflections and Treasury 

$UNIV reflections are distributed every hour. Your share of the reflection pool is proportional to the % of the total supply that you hold in your wallet.

TREASURY

The main objective of the treasury is to allocate part of the FEES to the investment of other projects such as NODES in order to make a more secure, stable and sustainable system by diversifying the origin of the rewards.

40% will be used by the protocol to invest In  other consolidated DeFi projects, like $STRONG or $UNIV nodes.

Sustainability Plan

Introducing our Sustainability Plan:
Atomverse bets on a long-term vision. We have seen lots of projects fail in very short time due to the lack of a consistent strategy
Guarantee a constant income to holders while ensuring the project's future is key.
In order to achieve this targets, we have ideated the following path:

1. 15% of each transaction will be charged as fee. Half of this amount will be directly sent to holders in $UNIV rewards.

2. Another 40% will be used by the protocol to invest In  other consolidated DeFi projects, like $STRONG or $UNIV nodes. The reason behind this is to ensure an alternative income source to holders, and this way don't have to entirely rely on trading fees.

3. The last 10% will be added to the liquidity pool to stabilize the price and reduce extreme volatilty.

4. At last, we plan to create our NFT nodes. Mint revenue will be also fully destinated to the protocol.
